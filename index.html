// 01 — ABOUT

MORE THAN
A SOCIETY.

We're a collective of students who care about computing — from first-years finding their feet to final-years shipping real projects.

Whether you're on an informatics course or just interested in tech, LeicesterCS connects you with like-minded people and gives you a platform to build, learn, and grow.

I.
BUILD

Hackathons, project nights, and sprints. Ship real things with real people.

II.
CONNECT

Industry talks, CV workshops, and a Discord that's actually active.

III.
PLAY

Game jams and our Minecraft SMP. Free to join, no membership needed.

IV.
LEARN

Workshops on Python, ML, Git, and whatever members want next.
